Investigate and analyze the socio-economic, environmental, and health impacts in Village.

Overview

Smart Fitness mirrors represent a revolutionary advancement in the integration of technology and everyday objects. I focused on understanding the challenges people face with traditional workouts and gym routines. From there, I developed features like voice control, touchscreens, and sensors to ensure the smart mirrors are not only functional but also enjoyable to use.
